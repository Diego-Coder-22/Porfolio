About Me

Hi, I'm Diego, a passionate Frontend Developer born in Montevideo, Uruguay, currently living in Granada, Spain.


I work as a Graphic Designer, leading a team of three designers to meet our clients' needs with creativity and precision. Previously, I held a similar role at BigPrints, Uruguay, where I gained valuable experience in graphic design and attention to detail.


In the last two years, I decided to shift my career path, diving into web development. I completed my studies as a Frontend Developer and am currently expanding my knowledge in Backend. This journey has allowed me to combine my graphic design experience with technical skills to offer complete and functional solutions.


I love working in teams, constantly learning, and tackling challenges that push me out of my comfort zone.
